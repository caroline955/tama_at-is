About us

Welcome to HAMT Pastry

Over the years, Hamt has grown from a small, humble bakery into a beloved brand trusted by many customers. Throughout this journey, we have stayed true to our passion for crafting cakes that are not only delicious but also carry a unique style, while constantly innovating to meet the diverse tastes of our guests.

At Hamt, we aim to bring you joy in every detail – from the balanced flavors, the elegant designs, to the thoughtful service.

What makes us most proud is not just the growth in our customer base or the reputation we have built, but the dedication, love, and passion of our bakers. We believe that every wonderful cake is created from a happy heart, and this is the value that Hamt will always cherish.
