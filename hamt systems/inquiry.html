ABOUT DELIVERY

We always try our best to ensure that the cake is delivered to you within the time frame you choose. However, there may be unexpected incidents due to traffic, weather, etc., leading to a slight delay in delivery. If the shipper does not arrive on time, please contact the hotline so we can assist you immediately.

The shipper will attempt to contact you 3 times. If unsuccessful, the cake will be returned to the store. You may arrange for a redelivery, but an additional shipping fee will apply.

Yes, you can change the delivery details if you notify us at least 4 hours before the scheduled delivery time. Please call our hotline for immediate assistance.

We recommend inspecting the cake immediately upon receipt. If you find any damage later, please take photos and send them to our hotline/email within 30 minutes of receipt for resolution.

ABOUT ORDERING

Yes, you can place orders via our Facebook Fanpage, Instagram DM, or by calling our hotline directly.

Standard cakes require at least 24 hours notice. Custom designs or large event orders should be placed 3-5 days in advance.

Changes to the cake type are allowed if the request is made at least 48 hours prior to the delivery date, subject to ingredient availability.

Cancellations made 48 hours in advance are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations within 24-48 hours may incur a 50% cancellation fee.

ABOUT PRODUCT

Our cream cakes should be kept refrigerated (2-5°C) and are best consumed within 48 hours. Cookies and dry pastries can be stored at room temperature for up to 7 days.

Our cakes are delicate. For car travel, keep the AC high and place the cake on a flat surface (floor). For motorbike, we strongly recommend using an insulated bag with ice packs if travel time exceeds 15 minutes.
